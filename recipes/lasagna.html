This is the recipe for Lasagna

Description

The lasagna described in this page is perfect for any weeknight dinner or holiday. It is also favoured by orange cats!

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams of pasta sheets
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 200 ml of heavy cream
  • 300 grams of minced meat
  • a healthy dose of love

Steps:

  1. Fry the minced meat until crispy, don't forget to add salt and pepper.
  2. Add the tomato paste, and the heavy cream. Cook for another 20 mintes.
  3. On a baking tray, layer the pasta sheets on the bottom, add the meat saunce. Repeat until the tray is full or out of meat sauce.
  4. Top the tray with cheese. (optional)
  5. Bake for 25 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius.
  6. Let rest for 15 minutes, and enjoy!
